Adopt shared formats and vocabularies across systems.

Part 5 · Frameworks and Models

From Prompt Engineering to Intent Engineering

  • Prompt engineering: optimize outputs one request at a time.
  • Intent engineering: design durable workflows around decision intent.
  • Institutional value: consistency across users, teams, and time.
