Barrie Robison

Workshop presenter

Credentials

Professor of Biology, Director of the Institute for Interdisciplinary Data Sciences, and Co-PI on the AI4RA NSF project.

Brief bio

Barrie Robison is a Professor of Biology at the University of Idaho and Director of the Institute for Interdisciplinary Data Sciences. He leads teams working across software development, databases, data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, genomics, bioinformatics, web and mobile app development, and high-performance computing. He has been an active researcher at the University of Idaho for more than 20 years and is actively engaged in deploying generative AI in higher education and research administration contexts.
