AI4RA: The Intersection Between AI and Data

A hands-on workshop about prompt engineering, research administration workflows, judgment, and where AI genuinely helps instead of merely creating noise.

Why this session

Human-centered AI for research administration

Artificial intelligence and data science are creating new ways to improve efficiency, uncover insight, and support strategic decision-making in higher education. This workshop helps attendees separate hype from practical value by showing how AI and data work together, where human expertise remains essential, and how to design workflows that are useful, reviewable, and grounded in real research administration work.

Learning objectives

What attendees should leave ready to do

Prompt with intent

Apply prompt engineering fundamentals to improve AI output quality, relevance, and consistency.

Decompose the work

Break complex research administration tasks into discrete, AI-manageable components.

Design end-to-end workflows

Build complete AI-assisted workflows for common research administration processes.

Judge fit and risk

Evaluate which tasks and workflows are suitable for AI deployment in participants' own institutions.
